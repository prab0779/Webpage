MEET OUR TEAM

Running the company since day one, Steve has an eye for web design and graphics. Since then he has been leading the company since day one. He is responsible for managing the daily operation.

Thomas has been working for Hot Beans for the last two years. He has studied web development at the University of Plymouth, since then he wrked at Apple. Thomas is able to design a website that looks eye-catching as well as fulfilling the client’s needs. He has a eye for design and colours and uses it to his advantage. Claire Oswald – Marketing Manager

Claire was hired by Hot Beans Web 2018. Having studied a marketing degree at the University of Manchester for 3 years, She knows the best methods of getting a business name out to the public. She is a valuable asset to Hot Beans Web and has helped us to secure over 20 clients over the last 2 years.


John Smith – Junior Developer

Recently finished his BTEC Level 3 IT course at the Royal National College for The Blind (RNC) in Hereford, John Smith has shown Hot Beans Web a huge potential of skills despite only finishing his IT course. He is very talented at software work and is very good at solving problem. Because John is also partially sighted, he helps to give us an insight into accessibility when we are designing and testing website. he is a very hardworking individual who works with enthusiasm and always finds a solution out of a problem.

Alex – Test Anaylst

Alex is responsible for testing our websites before they are launched for our clients. He has a keen eye for detail - after all, he is our quality control! Alex often works with our designers throughout the design process to make his testing process more efficient. Identifying the source of a bug can be as small as a single error in one line of code. He can see the big picture of a project and develop tests that can find these small errors. Alex loves all kinds of water sports, reading Science Fiction and finding the best Fish and Chips in the country.
